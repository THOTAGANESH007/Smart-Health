# Doctor Management System (**SMART HEALTH**)

## **Patient Module Features**

### **1. Registration & Profile Management**
- Secure sign-up/login via email or phone.
- Personal details: name, age, gender, contact info, address.
- Unique Patient ID / Health Card for identification.

### **2. Medical Records & History**
- Access doctor-uploaded prescriptions and lab results.
- Option to upload previous medical reports.
- ML-powered **health trend analysis** and predictive insights.

### **3. Appointment Booking**
- Search doctors by specialization, experience, and ratings.
- Book, reschedule, or cancel appointments.
- ML-based **smart scheduling** to reduce waiting times.

### **4. Telemedicine**
- Video/audio consultations with doctors.
- Symptom-based ML recommendations for suitable doctors.
- AI chatbot for health queries and basic treatment queries.

### **5. Prescription Management**
- Download prescriptions uploaded by doctors.
- ML-enabled **medication reminders** and **drug interaction alerts**.

### **6. Lab Tests & Reports**
- Schedule lab tests online.
- Track test status and download reports.
- ML-based **automated report analysis** for abnormal readings.

### **7. Notifications & Alerts**
- Appointment Reminders: SMS/email/in-app notifications.
- Prescription & Medication Alerts: ML-powered intelligent reminders.
- Health Tips: Personalized AI-generated health advice based on medical history.

### **8. Feedback & Ratings**
- Rate doctors and services.
- ML-based **sentiment analysis** to improve healthcare quality.


---

## **Doctor Module Features**

### **1. Registration & Profile Management**
- Secure doctor verification (medical license, specialization, experience).
- Manage availability, working hours, and consultation modes (in-person/online).
- View assigned patients and upcoming appointments.

### **2. Patient Interaction**
- Access patient medical history and uploaded reports.
- Add prescriptions, diagnoses, and treatment plans.
- Upload lab requests and view test results.

### **3. Appointment Management**
- Accept/reject/cancel appointments.
- Manage daily/weekly schedules.
- Set limits on maximum patients per day.

### **4. Telemedicine & Communication**
- Secure video/audio consultation with patients.
- Chat-based communication for quick queries.
- Option to share e-prescriptions instantly.

### **5. Prescription & Records**
- Digital prescription generation.
- Automatic drug interaction checker.
- Past prescription history for reference.

### **6. Notifications**
- Alerts for upcoming appointments.
- Updates when patients upload new reports.
- Reminders for pending prescriptions or follow-ups.

### **7. Feedback & Ratings**
- View patient ratings and reviews.
- ML-based feedback analytics for self-improvement.


---

## **Admin Module Features**

### **1. User & Role Management**
- Approve/reject doctor registrations (license verification).
- Manage patients, doctors, and receptionists.
- Assign roles and permissions.

### **2. Appointment & System Oversight**
- Monitor overall appointment statistics.
- Detect overbooked doctors and redistribute workload.
- Access analytics on patient-doctor interactions.

### **3. Data & Reports**
- Generate hospital/clinic-wide health reports.
- Export patient/doctor activity data.
- Dashboard with ML-powered predictive analytics.

### **4. Notifications & Communication**
- Send announcements to doctors/patients.
- Emergency alerts and health campaigns.
- Manage system-wide notifications.

### **5. Security & Compliance**
- Ensure HIPAA/GDPR compliance.
- Monitor suspicious activities and enforce data privacy.
- Role-based access control for sensitive data.

### **6. Feedback & Quality Monitoring**
- Monitor patient satisfaction levels.
- AI-powered sentiment analysis on reviews.
- Suggest system improvements to management.


---

## **Technology Stack**

- **Frontend:** React.js, Tailwind CSS  
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express.js  
- **Database:** MongoDB  
- **Authentication:** JWT / OAuth2  
- **ML Tools:** Python (scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch)  
- **Notifications:** Firebase / Twilio
